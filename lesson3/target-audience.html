Traget Audience

The audience that I will be targeting will be businessmen, government officials, and normal everyday individuals who would be looking for information regarding their chamber of commerce.

Personas

Ian

Ian is a county administrator looking to work with the Chamber of Commerce on projects around the county and city that they live in. He is a down-to-earth individual who works hard and wants to get to the point. He would like to get more information on meetings the Chamber may be having.

Kelly

Kelly is a mom who has a small business. She is wanting to attend the upcoming meeting regarding small businesses in the area. She is wondering if there is a place to sign up so that she makes sure she can get into the main conference room before it fills up.
